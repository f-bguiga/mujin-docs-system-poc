# Safety & Compliance Standards

The MujinController operates heavy industrial machinery. All integrators must adhere to **ISO 10218-1** safety requirements.

!!! danger "Immediate Physical Hazard"
    Never enter the robot's work envelope while the "Auto" light is illuminated. Failure to comply can result in severe injury or death.

### Safety Layers
1. **Mechanical:** Hard stops and safety fencing.
2. **Electronic:** Dual-channel Emergency Stop (E-Stop).
3. **Software:** MujinOS real-time collision avoidance.
